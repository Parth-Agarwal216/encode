2 Factor Authentication: Users report difficulties in setting up 2-factor authentication, with some experiencing glitches in the second step, preventing them from receiving the authentication code.
Chatbot for Customer Support: The chatbot's unresponsiveness and generation of irrelevant answers are noted issues, impacting the effectiveness of customer support.
Overall Service Quality: Positive feedback regarding consistent and stress-free financial transactions, showcasing the company's commitment to providing quality service.
General Satisfaction: While meeting basic needs, customers express a desire for the company to go above and beyond in various aspects.
